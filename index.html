About Me

I may look like I'm having really deep thoughts but 95% of the time I'm just thinking about gummy bears. Haribos to be exact! I am Breana Busby and I'm addicted to gummy bears! Born in Grand Rapids but raised in Dallas, Texas home of the Dallas Mavericks. Yes, you read that right. I am a product of the Art Institute of Dallas with a degree in Digital Filmmaking and Video Production. I specialize in Video Editing, Technical Directing, and soon to be Programming! A few other things I love is God, family, friends, music, movies, and computers.
